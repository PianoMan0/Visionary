# Visionary
About a year ago, I had an idea… about ideas. In the digital age, everyone had a killer idea for a project at some point. An app, a website, a game… but it needs to be made real. That’s where developers come in. But until now, there hasn’t been a way to connect the two - developers and idea creators.
<br><br>
Visionary was made to connect idea creators and developers. Anyone can sign up, post their idea, and a developer will claim it and create it. Your idea can be small or big, sort of vague or very detailed. No matter what, it matters.
Once your idea is posted, you can communicate with the developer through a public chat made just for your idea. This is recommended in the spirit of open source! But you can also chat with other users in DMs.
<br><br>
That’s not all. Visionary includes all the features that make this process as fun and easy as possible! You can search users, search ideas, customize your profile and visit the profile of others, check out the analytics of both your ideas and the site as a whole, report users, and customize your settings. Plus, if you ever have any questions while using the site, you can message an admin from your settings page!
<br><br>
This is my biggest project of all time by far. I am so excited for you to check it out. This will certainly be expanded in the future. If you ever have any questions, feel free to DM me @PianoMan0 on Slack!
